Our Story

Document management should be fast, free, and above all, private.

The PDFMitra Mission

PDFMitra was born out of a simple observation: Most PDF tools on the internet treat your data as a commodity. They require you to upload your sensitive documents to their servers, where you lose control over who sees them.

We built PDFMitra to change that. By using cutting-edge browser technologies, we've moved the entire processing engine to your device. When you use our tools, your files never leave your computer.

Privacy First

No uploads. No cloud. Just local processing for ultimate security.

Lightning Fast

No waiting for upload queues. Conversion happens instantly.
